<SUMMARY>
A 20-year-old Palestinian man, Shaaban al-Dalou, died in a hospital courtyard in Gaza after an Israeli strike.
-  He was in a hospital bed, connected to an IV drip, when the attack occurred.
-  Displaced people were sheltering in the courtyard at the time of the strike.
-  The strike ignited a fire that resulted in the young man's death.
-  The incident occurred in October 2024.
-  This event highlights the tragic consequences of the conflict in Gaza.
